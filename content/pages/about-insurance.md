We accept most insurances, including Scott and White. The most common include:

- Scott and White (some policies)
- Blue Cross and Blue Shield (**Note:** we are not accepting BCBS HMO)
- Great West
- United
- Aetna
- FirstCare and First Health
- Pacificare
- Medicaid — Superior, Chips, Molina (**Note:** we are not accepting any new patients with Medicaid)

**Before your first visit**, it's a good idea to contact your insurance provider to ensure that we are an in-network provider.

Don't have insurance for medical care? We have a discounted cash paying (no insurance) fee schedule as well. Call our office for information about prices.
